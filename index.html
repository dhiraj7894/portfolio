Forgotten Villa is an atmospheric horror game that immerses players in a haunting and mysterious experience. With immersive sound design, cinematic cutscenes, and refined lighting, it delivers a suspenseful journey where every shadow hides a secret. As development nears completion, Forgotten Villa promises a gripping horror experience filled with mystery and discovery.
