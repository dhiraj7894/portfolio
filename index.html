Constituents Ai And Technology Private Limited

I worked as a freelance VR developer for a studio that specialized in creating immersive educational experiences. Our main focus was on transforming traditional classroom topics into interactive virtual reality (VR) lessons for students from grades 8 to 10. We selected various science experiments from biology, chemistry, and physics and recreated them in VR.For example, one of the projects I worked on involved simulating a chemical reaction in a VR environment. I designed a virtual lab where users could interact with different test tubes and chemicals. When a user mixed two chemicals, the VR environment would simulate the chemical reaction. An audio guide would play simultaneously, explaining the experiment's process and outcome. Additionally, there was an electronic-level visual representation that showed how electrons were reacting during the chemical process, providing a deeper understanding of the molecular changes taking place.This approach allowed students to engage with educational content in a more hands-on and visually dynamic way, making learning more interactive and effective.
