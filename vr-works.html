Rock Wheel Trench Cutter

A rock wheel trench cutter is an excavator used to dig trenches in hard and rocky terrain. The machine is equipped with a rotating cutting wheel that is mounted on an articulated arm that is capable of cutting trenches in a wide variety of different shapes and sizes. The cutting wheel is powered by an electric motor and can be set to rotate at different speeds to suit the application.

Butterfly Valve

A butterfly valve consists of a handle or actuator connected to the valve stem via gears. It consists of the disc in the centre of the pipe hole and is attached to the stem, which is in close contact with the rubber seat ring to prevent it from leaking at both ends.

Hydraulic Steering System

Hydraulic steering system is a closed-loop system that uses pressurized hydraulic fluids for changing the wheel angle of the front wheels based on the steering angle. It contains a steering wheel, steering column, hydraulic pump driven by a belt, rotary control valve connected with fluid lines, hydraulic cylinder consisting of a piston, and a driver control mechanism (rack & pinion/steering gearboxes).
